EZ-A
Mid-Low Kick Point
High LaunchMid Spin757 MA
Firm butt and mid sections with a softer Ni-Ti alloy tip that allows controlled release through impact. Suited to players with a descending attack angle, or those who struggle to release the club. The tip construction gives a responsive snap at impact while the stiff mid and butt sections provide stability and feel of control.
Successor to VIZARD MA
EZ-P
Mid-High Kick Point
High LaunchLow Spin757 MP
Firm at both butt and tip, with a softer mid section that generates whip and a late release through impact. Draw bias achieved by extensive layering of ultra-high-strength Toray T1100G throughout. The distance-oriented option for a neutral delivery.
Successor to VIZARD MP
EZ-Z
High Kick Point
Mid LaunchLow Spin757 FZ
Softer at the butt, building to maximum firmness at the mid and tip. Rigid tip section built with layers of high-strength Toray M40X carbon fiber. For strong swingers who are fighting a ballooning flight or excess spin. Most workable of the three.
Successor to VIZARD FZ
