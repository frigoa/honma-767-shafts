VIZARD EZ vs. Fujikura VENTUS — Color Philosophy Compared

Both Honma and Fujikura use the same color system to communicate shaft character. Red signals the highest launch, Blue occupies the mid-high launch / low-spin position, and Black or Gunmetal represents the most penetrating, lowest-spin option. The profiles map to one another almost directly — though each brand achieves them through distinct proprietary materials and construction philosophies.

Note on materials: Honma uses Ni-Ti alloy (EZ-A tip), TORAYCA® T1100G (EZ-P mid bias), and TORAYCA® M40X (EZ-Z tip) to tune each shaft's character. Fujikura achieves similar outcomes through its proprietary multi-material bias core VeloCore+ technology. Same color language, different engineering paths.
